A Masterchef experience

Claire Lara made history in 2010 as the first woman to win Master chef the professionals Since then, her culinary journey has seen her prepare exquisite meals for a host of celebrities and sports personalities. A standout moment in her career was cooking for the late Queen and Prince Philip. Claire has also collaborated with renowned chefs, including Mary Berry, during the King’s coronation celebrations. Now, alongside her French husband, she continues to delight diners across the UK and Europe, bringing her exceptional skills to tables far and wide.
